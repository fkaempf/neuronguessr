How Scoring Works

Location max 5,000 pts

5000 × e−(6.6 × d / D)²

d = distance to actual position, D = brain diagonal (~1,050 µm)

Synapses max 5,000 pts

5000 × e−1.5 × |ln(guess / actual)|

Log-ratio: being 2x off costs the same at 100 or 100,000 synapses
